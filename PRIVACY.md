  ## EN                                                                                                                                                                                                        
                                                            
  MathHack is a children's education game developed by CKR Games.
                                                                                                                                                                                                               
  ### Data Collection
  MathHack does not collect, store, or share any personal data.                                                                                                                                                
                                                               
  ### Offline Usage
  The app works entirely offline. It does not require an internet connection and does not send any data to any server.
                                                                                                                      
  ### Local Storage                                                                                                                                                                                            
  Game progress (XP, level, badges, settings) is stored locally on your device only. This data never leaves your device.
                                                                                                                                                                                                               
  ### Children's Privacy                                    
  MathHack is designed for children aged 6-12. It is compliant with COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act). No personal information is collected from children or adults.
                                                                                                                                                                                   
  ### Third-Party Services                                                                                                                                                                                     
  MathHack does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.
